About Us

KIA Parcels is a parcel management platform developed in July 2024 for Kisumu International Airport by Kasuku Tech Junction, a technology company founded by Michael Otieno Kasuku in December 2023.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide efficient, reliable, and user-friendly parcel management solutions to ensure the seamless handling and tracking of parcels at Kisumu International Airport.

Our Vision

We envision becoming the leading provider of innovative technology solutions in the aviation industry, enhancing the overall efficiency and customer experience at airports.

Our Features

  • Send Parcel: Easily send parcels with our streamlined process.
  • Sent Parcels: Track and manage parcels you have sent.
  • Received Parcels: View and manage parcels you have received.
  • Help: Access our Contact Us, About Us, and FAQs sections for support and information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need assistance, please feel free to reach out to us through our Contact Us page.
